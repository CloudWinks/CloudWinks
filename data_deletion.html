Data Deletion Instructions

CloudWinks - Your Data, Your Control

Overview

At CloudWinks, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page provides clear instructions on how to request the deletion of your data from our platform. We are committed to processing your deletion requests promptly and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

What Data We Collect

When you use CloudWinks with social login, we may collect and store the following types of data:

  • Profile Information: Name, email address, profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Social platform user IDs, access tokens (encrypted)
  • Account Data: Username, preferences, settings
  • Usage Data: Application usage patterns, feature interactions
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, browser details
  • Files and Content: Any documents, images, or data you upload to the platform

Required Information

To process your deletion request efficiently, please provide:

  • Your email address associated with the CloudWinks account
  • The social login platform(s) you used (Google, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Your full name as it appears on your account
  • Any additional account identifiers you may have
  • Reason for deletion (optional, but helps us improve our service)
Identity Verification: For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. This may involve confirming ownership of the email address or social media accounts associated with your CloudWinks profile.

Deletion Process Timeline

What to Expect:

  • Immediate Acknowledgment: We'll confirm receipt of your request within 24 hours
  • Identity Verification: 1-3 business days (if required)
  • Data Deletion: 7-14 business days for complete removal
  • Confirmation: We'll notify you when deletion is complete

What Gets Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All personal data associated with your account
  • Your uploaded files, documents, and content
  • Social login credentials and tokens
  • Usage history and preferences
  • Any backup copies of your data

Data Retention Exceptions

Some data may be retained for limited periods due to:

  • Legal Requirements: Data required by law to be retained
  • Security Logs: Anonymized security and audit logs (without personal identifiers)
  • Financial Records: Transaction records required for accounting purposes
  • Dispute Resolution: Data necessary for ongoing legal proceedings

Third-Party Data

Please note that data deletion from CloudWinks does not automatically delete data from third-party platforms (Google, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn) that you used for authentication. To delete data from these platforms, you must contact them directly:

  • Google: Google Account Data & Privacy
  • Facebook: Facebook Data Deletion
  • X (Twitter): X Data Partnerships
  • LinkedIn: LinkedIn Data Privacy

Additional Rights

In addition to data deletion, you also have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
Questions or Concerns? If you have any questions about this data deletion process or our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact our privacy team. We're here to help and ensure your data rights are respected.
